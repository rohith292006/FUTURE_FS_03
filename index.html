Our story · Est. 1972

54 years strong — where Jaihindpuram meets after school.

Since 1972, Amudham Bakery has quietly become Madurai's favourite meeting point. Long evenings turned into longer conversations here, one plate of mushroom puffs and one slice of chocolate cake at a time. Regulars don't just come for the food — they come back for the corner table, the familiar smell of fresh bread, and a place that never made anyone feel like they were spending too little or asking for too much.

We still bake the same way we did when this all started: fresh every morning, priced fairly, and made for everyone — from students splitting a ₹10 puff to families ordering a birthday cake.
